Bio

Hi there! My name is Diego, and I'm an Electronics Engineer. I hold a PhD in Control and Systems Science from the University of Paris-Saclay and a PhD in Electrical Engineering from the Autonomous University of San Luis Potosí (UASLP).

My main research interests include, but is not limited to, the stability of dynamical systems with time-delay and the design of delay-based controllers.

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at KU Leuven, Belgium under the supervision of Prof. Wim Michiels.
